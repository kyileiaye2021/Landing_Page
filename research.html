Research

RESEARCH INTEREST

I have a strong passion for advancing multisensory agentic AI systems and interpreting their complex reasoning with linguistic tools to improve model intelligence and support reliable decision-making in high-stakes domains such as clinical tasks, autonomous vehicles, and climate change prediction. I am also interested in how the human brain produces intelligent behavior and how we may replicate intelligence in machines.

Keywords: Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, AI Agents, Cognitive Computational Neuroscience, Computational Pathology

CURRENT RESEARCH

Advised by Dr. Yonatan Winetraub at Stanford University School of Medicine, I contribute to a non-invasive approach for cancer detection using AI. My work focuses on developing vision-language models for Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) grayscale pathology images to improve non-invasive cancer diagnosis in clinical settings.
